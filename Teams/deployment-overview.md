1.  <span data-ttu-id="0b4f1-107">初期段階で技術的および法的な評価を実施し、組織で Microsoft Teams を実装するために必要な規制を特定、定義します。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="0b4f1-107">Technical and legal assessment in the early stages to identify and define what controls are required to implement Microsoft Teams in your organization.</span></span>
